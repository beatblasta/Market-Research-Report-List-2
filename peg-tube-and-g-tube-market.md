<p><p>A PEG tube, or percutaneous endoscopic gastrostomy tube, is a tube inserted through the abdominal wall into the stomach to provide nutrition to patients who are unable to eat normally. A G-tube, or gastrostomy tube, is a similar device that is inserted directly into the stomach for the same purpose. Both PEG tubes and G-tubes are commonly used in patients who have difficulty swallowing, suffer from chronic conditions that impair their ability to eat, or require long-term nutritional support.</p><p>The PEG Tube and G-tube Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, rising geriatric population, and growing awareness about enteral nutrition. Technological advancements in tube insertion techniques and materials are also driving market growth. Manufacturers are also focusing on producing versatile tubes that can be used for a wide range of patient populations and medical conditions. Overall, the PEG Tube and G-tube Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years due to the increasing demand for enteral feeding solutions.</p></p>
