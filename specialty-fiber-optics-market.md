<p><p>Multimode fiber optics are designed to carry multiple light signals at the same time, allowing for faster data transmission over short distances. This type of fiber optics is commonly used in LAN networks and data centers. On the other hand, singlemode fiber optics are used for long-distance communication as they can carry a single light signal over greater distances with minimal signal loss. They are typically used in telecom networks and high-speed data transmission systems. Both types play essential roles in various industries for efficient data transmission.</p></p>
