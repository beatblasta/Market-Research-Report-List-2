<p><strong>The Low-E Glass Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sputtered (Soft Coating)</li><li>Pyrolytic (Hard Coating)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low-E glass coatings are categorized into two types: Sputtered (soft coating) and Pyrolytic (hard coating). Sputtered coatings are applied through a vacuum chamber process, offering superior energy efficiency and clarity. Pyrolytic coatings are applied during the float glass manufacturing process, providing durability and strength. Sputtered coatings are preferred for their flexibility in design and customization, while pyrolytic coatings are known for their durability and ease of maintenance. Both types enhance energy efficiency, reduce glare, and improve overall comfort within buildings.</p></p>
