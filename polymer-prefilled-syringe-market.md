<p><p>Polymer prefilled syringe market types include COP (Cyclic Olefin Polymer) and COC (Cyclic Olefin Copolymer). COP syringes offer excellent chemical resistance and transparency, making them ideal for sensitive drug formulations. COC syringes are known for their high heat resistance and low protein adsorption, making them suitable for biologics and vaccines. Other polymer types in the market may include polypropylene and polyethylene, which offer their own unique properties and benefits for prefilled syringe applications. Overall, polymer prefilled syringes provide a wide range of options for pharmaceutical packaging solutions.</p></p>
