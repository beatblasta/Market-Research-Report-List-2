<p><ul><li>Brushed</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile electronic power steering systems can be categorized into two types: brushed and brushless. Brushed systems use brushes and a commutator to control the flow of electric current, while brushless systems do not have brushes and instead use electronic sensors to control the current. Brushed systems are typically less expensive and simpler in design, while brushless systems are more efficient and require less maintenance. Both types offer advantages and disadvantages depending on the specific application and requirements of the automotive industry.</p></p>
