<p><strong>The Glow Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant</li><li>Tap Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glow Tube market can be categorized into two main types: Ferroresonant and Tap Switching. Ferroresonant Glow Tubes are designed to operate using the principle of ferroresonance, where the magnetic field produced by the transformer helps maintain a steady glow discharge. On the other hand, Tap Switching Glow Tubes utilize a tap switching mechanism to regulate the voltage and current flow, allowing for precise control over the glow discharge. Both types of Glow Tubes offer unique benefits and are used in various applications where reliable and stable glow discharge is required.</p></p>
