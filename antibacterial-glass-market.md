<p><p>Antibacterial glass markets consist of various types such as soluble antimicrobial glass, porous antimicrobial glass, coated antibacterial glass, and ion diffusion antibacterial glass. Soluble antimicrobial glass releases antimicrobial agents into the surrounding environment, while porous antimicrobial glass contains microorganisms within its structure. Coated antibacterial glass has a special coating that inhibits bacterial growth, and ion diffusion antibacterial glass releases ions that prevent bacteria from thriving. Each type of antibacterial glass offers unique benefits in inhibiting the growth of harmful bacteria on surfaces.</p></p>
