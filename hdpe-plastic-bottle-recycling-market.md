<p><p>HDPE Plastic Bottle Recycling Market includes two main types - Chemical Recycling and Mechanical Recycling. Chemical Recycling involves breaking down plastic bottles into their chemical components to be reused in new products. This process is efficient in removing contaminants and producing high-quality recycled materials. On the other hand, Mechanical Recycling involves shredding and melting plastic bottles to create new products. This method is simpler and more cost-effective but may result in lower quality recycled materials. Both types contribute to the growing market for HDPE plastic bottle recycling.</p></p>
