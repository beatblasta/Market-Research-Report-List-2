<p><strong>The Brushless AC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Brushless AC Motors</li><li>Outer Rotor Brushless AC Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brushless AC motors are classified into two main types: Inner Rotor and Outer Rotor motors. Inner Rotor motors have the rotor located inside the stator, which results in a more compact design and higher power density. On the other hand, Outer Rotor motors have the rotor located outside the stator, offering higher torque capability and lower cogging. Both types have their unique advantages and are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation.</p></p>
