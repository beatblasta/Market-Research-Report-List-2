<p><p>Piano lights come in various types to cater to different lighting needs and preferences. Tabletop piano lights are compact and sit on top of the piano, providing focused lighting for sheet music. Floor standing piano lights are taller and provide adjustable lighting at a higher level. Clip-on piano lights attach directly to the music stand for easy adjustment and positioning. Other types of piano lights include wall-mounted or overhead options, offering unique lighting solutions for pianists. Each type offers a different lighting experience for practicing or performing on the piano.</p></p>
