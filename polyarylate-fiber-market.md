<p><p>Polyarylate fiber market can be classified into solid and hollow types. Solid polyarylate fibers have a uniform thickness throughout their length, providing consistent strength and durability. On the other hand, hollow polyarylate fibers have an empty space in the center, making them lighter and more flexible. Solid fibers are commonly used in applications where strength is paramount, such as ropes and cables, while hollow fibers are preferred in applications where weight and flexibility are more important, such as sports equipment and clothing.</p></p>
