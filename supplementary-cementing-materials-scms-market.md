<p><p>Supplementary Cementing Materials (SCMs) are materials that can be added to concrete mixtures to enhance performance and sustainability. These materials include Fly Ash, Slag Cement, Silica Fume, and Quartz Flour. Fly Ash is a byproduct of coal combustion, Slag Cement is a byproduct of steel production, Silica Fume is a byproduct of silicon metal manufacturing, and Quartz Flour is a finely ground silica material. These SCMs improve concrete durability, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and decrease the need for virgin raw materials.</p></p>
