<p><ul><li>TFT Type</li><li>TN Type</li><li>STN Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polarizer film market types include TFT (Thin Film Transistor) type, TN (Twisted Nematic) type, and STN (Super Twisted Nematic) type. TFT type polarizers are commonly used in LCD panels for electronic devices due to their high resolution and image quality. TN type polarizers are known for their fast response time and low cost, making them popular for gaming and multimedia applications. STN type polarizers offer improved image quality and wider viewing angles compared to TN type, making them suitable for industrial and medical displays.</p></p>
