<p><ul><li>Helium-flow</li><li>Liquid Helium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of vacuum cryostats commonly used in the market: helium-flow and liquid helium cryostats. Helium-flow cryostats cool samples using a flow of helium gas, while liquid helium cryostats use liquid helium to achieve ultra-low temperatures. Helium-flow cryostats are often more cost-effective and easier to maintain, making them a popular choice for many research applications. On the other hand, liquid helium cryostats are known for their superior cooling capabilities and are commonly used for experiments requiring very low temperatures.</p></p>
