<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Switching diodes are available in two main market types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Through Hole. SMD switching diodes are smaller in size, making them suitable for use in compact electronic devices where space is limited. On the other hand, Through Hole switching diodes are larger and are often used in applications where durability and stability are important. Both types of switching diodes play a crucial role in electronic circuits by allowing current to flow in only one direction, making them essential components in various electronic devices.</p></p>
