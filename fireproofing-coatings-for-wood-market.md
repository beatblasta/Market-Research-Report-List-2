<p><p>Fireproofing coatings for wood are essential for protecting wood surfaces from catching fire and spreading flames. Two common types of fireproofing coatings on the market are water-based and solvent-based. Water-based coatings are non-toxic, environmentally friendly, and easy to clean up with water. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings provide a stronger bond to the wood surface and are often preferred for outdoor applications where durability is critical. Both options offer effective fire protection for wooden surfaces in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
