<p><p>Threaded pipe fittings are available in two main types, internal thread and external thread. Internal thread fittings have threads on the inside of the fitting and are used to connect two pipes with male ends. External thread fittings have threads on the outside of the fitting and are used to connect two pipes with female ends. Both types of fittings are commonly used in plumbing and mechanical applications to create secure and leak-proof connections between pipes.</p></p>
