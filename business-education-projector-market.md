<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Liquid-Crystal Display (LCD)</li><li>Light-Emitting Diode (LED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for business education projectors can be categorized into three main types: Digital Light Processing (DLP), Liquid-Crystal Display (LCD), and Light-Emitting Diode (LED). DLP projectors use microscopic mirrors to reflect light and produce images, offering high contrast ratios and fast response times. LCD projectors use liquid crystal technology to create images, providing high color accuracy and sharpness. LED projectors use light-emitting diodes as a light source, offering long-lasting and energy-efficient performance. Each type has its own unique advantages and features to cater to different business and educational needs.</p></p>
