<p><p>Pre-scored ampules are glass containers that are scored or pre-cut for easy and safe opening. They come in two main types - Amber Ampules and Clear Ampules. Amber Ampules are designed to protect light-sensitive medications from degradation caused by exposure to light. Clear Ampules, on the other hand, are transparent and allow for easy visibility of the contents inside. Both types of pre-scored ampules are essential packaging solutions for pharmaceutical and healthcare industries to ensure the safety and integrity of their products.</p></p>
