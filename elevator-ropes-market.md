<p><p>Elevator ropes are essential components in various types of elevators, including traction elevators, hydraulic elevators, and machine-room-less (MRL) elevators. In traction elevators, ropes are used to move the elevator car up and down by gripping a sheave or pulley. Hydraulic elevators utilize ropes to support the elevator car while the hydraulic system provides the lifting force. In MRL elevators, ropes are used for both supporting the elevator car and providing the lifting mechanism, allowing for a more compact and efficient design.</p></p>
