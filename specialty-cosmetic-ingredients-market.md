<p><p>The Specialty Cosmetic Ingredients Market includes various types such as Emollients, Surfactants, Anionics, Non-Anionics, Cationics, Amphoterics, and others. Emollients are used for softening and smoothing the skin, while Surfactants help in cleansing and foaming. Anionics are negatively charged surfactants, Non-Anionics are neutral surfactants, and Cationics are positively charged surfactants. Amphoterics act as both anionic and cationic surfactants. Other specialty cosmetic ingredients include antioxidants, preservatives, and UV filters, among others.</p></p>
