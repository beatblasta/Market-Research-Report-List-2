<p><ul><li>Motor/Generator</li><li>Battery</li><li>Power Electronics Controller</li><li>Converter</li><li>Transmission</li><li>On-board Charger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric powertrain systems consist of various components essential for the functioning of electric vehicles. The motor/generator component is responsible for converting electrical energy into mechanical energy, while batteries store and provide power to the system. The power electronics controller regulates the flow of electricity, while the converter converts the electrical energy from the battery to a form usable by the motor/generator. The transmission component is responsible for transferring power from the motor to the wheels, and the on-board charger is used to recharge the batteries.</p></p>
