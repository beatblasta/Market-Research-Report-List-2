<p><p>Polycarbonate polyols can be classified into two main types: liquid and solid. Liquid polycarbonate polyols are typically used in the production of flexible foams, coatings, adhesives, and sealants. They are preferred for their ease of handling and blending with other materials. On the other hand, solid polycarbonate polyols are commonly used in the manufacturing of rigid foams, coatings, and elastomers. They offer improved mechanical properties and can be easily processed into various forms such as pellets or powders. Both types play a significant role in the polycarbonate polyol market.</p></p>
