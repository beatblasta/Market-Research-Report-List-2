<p><p>Gyro compasses are navigation instruments used to determine direction based on the Earth's rotation. There are two main types of gyro compasses: fiber optic and mechanical. Fiber optic gyro compasses use light waves to detect orientation changes, offering higher accuracy and reliability. Mechanical gyro compasses, on the other hand, rely on mechanical components to maintain orientation, making them less accurate but more cost-effective. Both types are essential tools for navigation in various industries such as maritime, aviation, and defense.</p></p>
