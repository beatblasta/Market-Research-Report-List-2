<p><strong>The Electrodialysis Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Membranes</li><li>Homogeneous Membranes</li><li>Bipolar Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrodialysis membranes are classified into three main types: heterogeneous membranes, homogeneous membranes, and bipolar membranes. Heterogeneous membranes consist of different layers with varying properties, allowing for selective ion transport. Homogeneous membranes have a uniform structure and composition, providing consistent ion selectivity. Bipolar membranes have both anion and cation exchange layers, enabling the splitting of water molecules into hydrogen and hydroxide ions. Each type of membrane is designed for specific applications in electrodialysis processes, catering to different requirements for ion separation and purification.</p></p>
