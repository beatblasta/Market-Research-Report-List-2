<p><p>Sleeve applicator market consists of two main types: linear applicator and rotary applicator. Linear applicators are typically used for high-speed production lines where sleeves are applied in a straight line motion. On the other hand, rotary applicators are used for slower production lines where sleeves are applied in a circular motion. Both types of sleeve applicators offer precise and efficient application of sleeves onto products, catering to different production needs and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
