<p><p>Rubber repair glue market is segmented into two main types: hot bond and cold bond. Hot bond repair involves using a heat source to cure the adhesive, creating a strong and durable bond. Cold bond repair, on the other hand, does not require any heat and relies on chemical reactions to create the bond. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on the specific requirements of the repair job.</p></p>
