<p><p>Guard Locking Switches are widely used in industrial settings to ensure safety by preventing access to hazardous areas when machinery is in operation. There are two main types of Guard Locking Switches: Electrical and Mechanical. Electrical Guard Locking Switches rely on an electrical signal to secure the guard in place, while Mechanical Guard Locking Switches use physical mechanisms such as key locks or bolts. Both types play a critical role in maintaining a safe work environment and preventing accidents.</p></p>
