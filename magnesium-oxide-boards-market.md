<p><p>Magnesium oxide boards are categorized into three main types based on their thickness: thin (under 8 mm), medium (8-15 mm), and thick (above 15 mm). Thin boards are typically used in projects where weight is a concern, such as ceiling applications. Medium boards are commonly used for wall partitions and cladding. Thick boards are well-suited for heavy-duty applications like flooring and fire-resistant barriers. Each type caters to specific construction requirements and offers unique benefits in terms of strength, durability, and insulation properties.</p></p>
