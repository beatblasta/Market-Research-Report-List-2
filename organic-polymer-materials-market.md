<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Polymer Material</li><li>Thermosetting Polymer Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic polymer materials are divided into two main market types: thermoplastic polymer materials and thermosetting polymer materials. Thermoplastic polymer materials can be melted and reshaped multiple times without losing their properties, making them versatile and recyclable. On the other hand, thermosetting polymer materials undergo a chemical reaction during curing, resulting in a rigid structure that cannot be reshaped. Thermosetting materials offer high heat and chemical resistance, making them suitable for applications where durability is essential. Both types play critical roles in various industries.</p></p>
