<p><p>Silicone Gum Blend is primarily categorized into three types based on viscosity levels - Low Viscosity, Medium Viscosity, and High Viscosity. Low Viscosity blends are thinner and flow easily, making them suitable for applications requiring a softer texture. Medium Viscosity blends offer a balance between flowability and thickness, ideal for a wide range of applications. High Viscosity blends are thicker and more resistant to flow, making them suitable for applications where a strong binding or thickening agent is needed. Each type caters to specific requirements in the market.</p></p>
