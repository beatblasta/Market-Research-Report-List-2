<p><strong>The Ceramic Foundry Sand Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Casting</li><li>Mold Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic foundry sand is widely used in core casting and mold casting applications in the foundry industry. In core casting, the sand is shaped into a core that is placed in the mold to create cavities within the final casting. This helps in achieving intricate and complex designs. In mold casting, the sand is used to create the mold into which molten metal is poured to create the final casting. This process is efficient and cost-effective, making ceramic foundry sand a popular choice in the industry.</p></p>
