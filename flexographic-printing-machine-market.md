<p><strong>The Flexographic Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unit -type Machine</li><li>Central Impression Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexographic Printing Machine market can be categorized into two types: Unit-type Machine and Central Impression Machine. </p><p>Unit-type Machines have separate printing units for each color, allowing for flexibility in printing different colored designs. </p><p>Central Impression Machines, on the other hand, have a single, large cylinder that rotates and brings all colors in contact with the printing surface simultaneously, resulting in higher printing speeds and better registration accuracy. Both types cater to different printing requirements and offer unique advantages in the market.</p></p>
