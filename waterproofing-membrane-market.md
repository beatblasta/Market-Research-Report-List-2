<p><strong>The Waterproofing Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modified Bitumen Membrane</li><li>Synthetic Polymer Waterproofing Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified Bitumen Membrane: This type of waterproofing membrane is made of bitumen that has been modified with polymers such as styrene-butadiene-styrene (SBS) or atactic polypropylene (APP). These membranes provide excellent flexibility, durability, and resistance to UV rays. They are commonly used in low-slope roofing systems.</p><p>Synthetic Polymer Waterproofing Membrane: This type of membrane is made of synthetic polymers such as PVC, TPO, or EPDM. These membranes are known for their high tensile strength, flexibility, and resistance to chemicals and UV rays. They are widely used in both roofing and below-grade applications for their long-lasting performance.</p></p>
