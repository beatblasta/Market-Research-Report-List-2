<p><p>Suction coagulators are medical devices used during surgical procedures to simultaneously suction fluids and cauterize tissue to control bleeding. There are two main types of suction coagulators on the market: handswitch and footswitch. Handswitch suction coagulators are controlled by hand, allowing for more precise movements and control during surgery. Footswitch suction coagulators, on the other hand, are controlled using foot pedals, providing surgeons with hands-free operation and increased efficiency during procedures.</p></p>
