<p><p>Implantable Ventricular Assist Devices (IVADs) are devices used to help support heart function in patients with severe heart failure. There are three main types of IVADs: Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVADs), Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVADs), and Biventricular Assist Devices (BIVADs). LVADs help support the left side of the heart, RVADs support the right side, and BIVADs support both sides. Each type of IVAD is designed to help improve heart function and overall quality of life for patients with severe heart failure.</p></p>
