<p><ul><li>Expansive Clay</li><li>Expanded Shale</li><li>Sintered Fly Ash</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lightweight aggregates market includes various types such as expansive clay, expanded shale, sintered fly ash, and others. Expansive clay aggregates are known for their expansion properties upon exposure to moisture, making them suitable for construction applications. Expanded shale aggregates are lightweight and durable, ideal for insulating and structural purposes. Sintered fly ash aggregates are created by sintering fly ash particles at high temperatures, offering good strength and thermal insulation. Other types of lightweight aggregates include pumice, perlite, and lightweight aggregate made from recycled materials.</p></p>
