<p><ul><li>Iron-based</li><li>Fe-Ni-based</li><li>Cobalt-based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amorphous soft magnetic materials are categorized into four main types based on their composition: iron-based, Fe-Ni-based, cobalt-based, and others. Iron-based materials typically offer high saturation magnetization and low coercivity. Fe-Ni-based materials are known for their high permeability and low core loss. Cobalt-based materials have high saturation magnetization and excellent chemical stability. The "others" category includes a variety of compositions such as Fe-Si-B, Fe-Co-Si-B, and Fe-Co-Nb. Each type of material has unique properties that make them suitable for specific applications in industries like electronics and power generation.</p></p>
