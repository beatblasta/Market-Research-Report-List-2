<p><p>Continuous carbon fiber, which is long, unbroken strands of carbon fibers, is typically used in high-performance applications such as aerospace and automotive industries due to its superior strength and stiffness. Long carbon fiber consists of carbon fibers with lengths between continuous and short fibers, offering a balance between strength and cost-effectiveness. Short carbon fiber, with lengths ranging from a few millimeters to centimeters, is commonly used in industries like electronics and sporting goods where high strength and lightweight properties are desired at a lower cost.</p></p>
