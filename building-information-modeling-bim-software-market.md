<p><p>Building Information Modeling (BIM) software is a tool used in the architecture, engineering, and construction industry to create and manage digital representations of physical and functional characteristics of buildings and infrastructure. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period.</p><p>Key trends driving the growth of the BIM software market include the increasing demand for cloud-based BIM solutions, integration of BIM software with other technologies such as virtual reality and IoT, and the rising popularity of 4D and 5D BIM for integrated project planning and cost estimation.</p><p>Moreover, the emergence of advanced BIM software features like clash detection, energy analysis, and facility management is further fueling market growth. The demand for BIM software is also being driven by government regulations mandating the use of BIM in public infrastructure projects to improve efficiency and reduce costs.</p></p>
