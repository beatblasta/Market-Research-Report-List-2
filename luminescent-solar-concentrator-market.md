<p><ul><li>Reflective condenser</li><li>Refracting condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Luminescent Solar Concentrators are available in two main types: reflective condenser and refracting condenser. Reflective condenser systems use mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a luminescent material, which then converts the light into electricity. On the other hand, refracting condenser systems use lenses to focus sunlight onto the luminescent material. Both types of concentrators increase the efficiency of solar panels by concentrating sunlight onto a smaller area, thus maximizing the energy output.</p></p>
