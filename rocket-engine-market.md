<p><ul><li>Liquid Rocket Engine</li><li>Solid Rocket Engine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rocket engine market is segmented into two main types - Liquid Rocket Engine and Solid Rocket Engine. Liquid rocket engines use liquid propellants that are stored in tanks separately and mixed together in combustion chamber. They offer higher thrust and efficiency, making them suitable for space missions. On the other hand, solid rocket engines use solid propellants packed into a casing, making them simpler in design and more reliable. They are commonly used in military missiles and launch vehicles. Both types cater to different needs in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
