<p><p>The common mode noise filter market includes two primary types: Through Hole and Surface Mount Device (SMD). Through Hole filters are soldered onto the circuit board through holes, providing a secure and stable connection. SMD filters are smaller and lighter, making them ideal for compact electronic devices. Both types offer effective noise suppression by filtering out unwanted signals that can disrupt the performance of sensitive electronic equipment. Both Through Hole and SMD filters cater to different requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
