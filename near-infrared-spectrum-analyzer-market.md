<p><p>The Near-Infrared Spectrum Analyzer market consists of different types of analyzers including FT-NIR Analyzer, Dispersive NIR Analyzer, and Others like AOTF and Filter-based analyzers. FT-NIR analyzers use Fourier transform technology to provide precise and accurate measurements, while dispersive NIR analyzers use diffraction grating to separate light into different wavelengths. Other analyzers such as AOTF and filter-based analyzers use acousto-optic tunable filters or specific filters to analyze near-infrared spectra for various applications.</p></p>
