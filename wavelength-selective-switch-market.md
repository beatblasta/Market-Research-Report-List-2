<p><strong>The Wavelength Selective Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LCOS based Devices</li><li>MEMS based Devices</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wavelength Selective Switch market consists of different types of devices, including LCOS based devices, MEMS based devices, and others. LCOS based devices utilize liquid crystal on silicon technology to control light wavelengths, offering high performance and precise wavelength selection. MEMS based devices use micro-electromechanical systems for wavelength control, providing fast response times and high reliability. The "Others" market category includes a variety of other technologies used for wavelength selective switching, such as acousto-optic devices and fiber Bragg gratings.</p></p>
