<p><p>Optical energy meters are classified into different types based on their mechanism of energy conversion. The Pyroelectric energy meters operate by measuring the change in temperature caused by the absorption of optical energy. Photodiode energy meters, on the other hand, convert optical energy directly into electrical current. There are also other types of optical energy meters that utilize different mechanisms for energy detection. Each type of meter has its own advantages and applications in various industries where precise energy measurement is required.</p></p>
