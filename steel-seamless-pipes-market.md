<p><ul><li>Hot-rolled Steel Seamless Pipes</li><li>Cold-rolled Steel Seamless Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel Seamless Pipes are available in two main types - hot-rolled and cold-rolled. Hot-rolled Steel Seamless Pipes are manufactured using heat to deform the steel into a cylindrical shape without the need for welding. This process results in a rough surface finish and is commonly used for structural and industrial applications. On the other hand, Cold-rolled Steel Seamless Pipes are processed at room temperature, resulting in a smoother surface finish that is ideal for precision engineering and high-performance applications.</p></p>
