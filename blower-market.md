<p><p>Blower markets are categorized into three types based on pressure levels: Low Pressure, Medium Pressure, and High Pressure. Low-pressure blowers are used for applications that require air movement at a low pressure, such as HVAC systems. Medium-pressure blowers are used in applications that require air movement at a moderate pressure level, such as drying and conveying systems. High-pressure blowers are designed for applications that require air movement at a high pressure level, such as pneumatic conveying and air filtration systems.</p></p>
