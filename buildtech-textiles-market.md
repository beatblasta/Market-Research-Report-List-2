<p><ul><li>Woven Fabric</li><li>Non-Woven Fabric</li><li>Knitted Fabric</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buildtech textiles market can be categorized into four main types - woven fabric, non-woven fabric, knitted fabric, and others. Woven fabric is created by interlacing two sets of yarns at right angles, providing strength and durability. Non-woven fabric involves fibers bonded together without being woven, offering versatility and cost-effectiveness. Knitted fabric is made by interconnecting loops of yarn, providing elasticity and flexibility. The "Others" category includes innovative fabric types such as coated fabrics, laminates, and composite materials.</p></p>
