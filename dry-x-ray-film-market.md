<p><p>Dry X-ray film market consists of three main types: Laser Imaging Film, Thermal Sensitive Film, and Inkjet Film. Laser imaging film is photosensitive and produces high-resolution images using laser technology. Thermal sensitive film uses heat to create images, making it cost-effective and efficient. Inkjet film, on the other hand, utilizes inkjet printing technology to produce X-ray images, offering flexibility and convenience. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the medical imaging industry.</p></p>
