<p><strong>The Post-Applied Waterproofing Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sheet-based Membrane</li><li>Liquid Applied Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Post-applied waterproofing membrane market consists of two main types: sheet-based membrane and liquid applied membrane. Sheet-based membranes are pre-fabricated membranes that are rolled out onto the surface and joined together. They are durable and easy to install. Liquid applied membranes, on the other hand, are applied as a liquid, which dries to form a seamless waterproofing layer. They are flexible and can conform to irregular surfaces. Both types provide effective protection against water infiltration in various construction applications.</p></p>
