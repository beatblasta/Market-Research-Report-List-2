<p><p>The Chronic Total Occlusion Devices Market includes various types of devices such as Micro Catheters, Guide Wires, Crossing Devices, and Re-entry Devices. Micro Catheters are used to deliver devices to the occlusion site, Guide Wires are used to navigate through the blocked artery, Crossing Devices are used to penetrate the occlusion, and Re-entry Devices help re-enter the true lumen. These devices are crucial in treating Chronic Total Occlusions and restoring blood flow in blocked arteries.</p></p>
