<p><p>Gray Iron Castings Market can be categorized into two main types based on the molding process used for production: Vertical Molding and Horizontal Molding market. In Vertical Molding, the mold is placed vertically and metal is poured from the top, allowing for better control over the flow of metal and reducing defects. In Horizontal Molding, the mold is placed horizontally and metal is poured from the side, making it suitable for larger and more complex casting shapes. These two markets cater to different casting requirements and industries.</p></p>
