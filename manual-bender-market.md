<p><p>There are two main types of manual benders in the market: Mechanical Manual Bending Machines and Electric Manual Bending Machines. Mechanical manual benders are operated by hand and rely on physical force to bend metal or other materials. Electric manual benders, on the other hand, use electric power to assist in the bending process, making them easier to use for larger or tougher materials. Both types serve the same purpose of bending materials, but the method of operation and power source differ between the two.</p></p>
