<p><p>Fractionated plasma products are typically classified into three main types: coagulation factors, immune globulins, and albumin. </p><p>Coagulation factors are essential for blood clotting and are used to treat hemophilia and other clotting disorders. </p><p>Immune globulins are antibodies that help boost the immune system and are used to treat autoimmune diseases and infections. </p><p>Albumin is a protein that helps maintain blood volume and is often used to treat conditions like shock and burns.</p><p>Other fractionated plasma products may include enzymes, hormones, and growth factors used for various medical conditions.</p></p>
