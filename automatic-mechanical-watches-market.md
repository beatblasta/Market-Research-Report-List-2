<p><ul><li>Hand Chain Mechanical Watch</li><li>Automatic Chain - up Hand Mechanical Watch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic mechanical watches can be classified into two main types - hand chain mechanical watches and automatic chain-up hand mechanical watches. Hand chain mechanical watches require manual winding by turning the crown to power the watch. On the other hand, automatic chain-up hand mechanical watches are self-winding, utilizing the movement of the wearer's wrist to wind the watch's mainspring. Both types cater to different preferences and offer unique benefits, making them popular choices in the watch market.</p></p>
