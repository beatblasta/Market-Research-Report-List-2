<p><p>Soy Dietary Fibers Market Types are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their characteristics. Type I Soy Dietary Fibers are mainly insoluble fibers derived from soybean hulls, which are known for their ability to improve bowel health and prevent constipation. On the other hand, Type II Soy Dietary Fibers are soluble fibers extracted from the soy protein isolates, which are beneficial in lowering cholesterol levels and regulating blood sugar levels.
