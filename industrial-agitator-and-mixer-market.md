<p><p>Industrial agitators and mixers are used in various industries for mixing liquids, suspending solids, and promoting chemical reactions. The market is divided into different types based on the entry point of the agitator or mixer. Top entry agitators and mixers are commonly used for high viscosity fluids, while side entry ones are used for blending fluids in tanks. Bottom entry agitators are ideal for applications requiring low to medium viscosity. Other types may include portable mixers, static mixers, and magnetic mixers, among others.</p></p>
