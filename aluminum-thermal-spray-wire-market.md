<p><p>Aluminum thermal spray wire is a type of wire used in thermal spray processes to apply a protective coating onto a substrate. This process is commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p><p>The aluminum thermal spray wire market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period.
