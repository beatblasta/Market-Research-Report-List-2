<p><p>Direct roving is a type of fiber reinforcement material widely used in the production of composite materials. It is made up of continuous glass filament strands that are coated with a sizing agent to improve adhesion to the matrix material. Direct roving provides excellent mechanical properties, such as high strength and stiffness, making it ideal for applications in industries like construction, automotive, aerospace, and wind energy.</p><p>The direct roving market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for lightweight and high-performance materials in various end-use industries. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period. One of the key trends in the direct roving market is the rising adoption of advanced composite materials in automotive and aerospace applications to improve fuel efficiency and reduce carbon emissions. Additionally, the growing focus on renewable energy sources, such as wind power, is driving the demand for direct roving in the production of wind turbine blades.</p><p>Overall, the direct roving market is poised for steady growth, fueled by technological advancements, increasing investments in infrastructure development, and the shifting preference towards sustainable and eco-friendly materials.</p></p>
