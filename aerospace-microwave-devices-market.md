<p><p>The Aerospace Microwave Devices market is segmented into Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) and Extremely High Frequency (EHF) types. UHF refers to frequencies between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, commonly used for long-range communication and radar systems. Whereas EHF includes frequencies ranging from 30 GHz to 300 GHz, suitable for short-range communication and satellite communication systems. Both UHF and EHF technologies play crucial roles in aerospace applications, offering unique advantages for different types of communication and radar systems.</p></p>
