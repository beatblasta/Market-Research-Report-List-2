<p><p>Positron Emission Computed Tomography (PET) market includes three main types: Direct Imaging, Indirect Imaging, and Alternative Imaging. Direct Imaging involves directly measuring the annihilation photons produced by positron-electron collisions. Indirect Imaging includes imaging techniques that indirectly detect the emitted gamma rays. Alternative Imaging methods, such as prompt gamma imaging, offer alternative approaches for PET imaging. Each type offers unique advantages and limitations in terms of image quality, resolution, and sensitivity, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
