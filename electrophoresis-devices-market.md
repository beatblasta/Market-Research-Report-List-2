<p><p>The Electrophoresis Devices Market can be classified into two main types: Gel Electrophoresis Systems and Capillary Electrophoresis Systems. Gel Electrophoresis Systems involve separating molecules based on size using a gel matrix, while Capillary Electrophoresis Systems separate molecules based on charge and size using a capillary tube. Gel Electrophoresis Systems are widely used for DNA and protein analysis, while Capillary Electrophoresis Systems are known for their high resolution and sensitivity, making them ideal for complex mixture analysis.</p></p>
