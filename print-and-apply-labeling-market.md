<p><p>Print and Apply Labeling refers to the type of labeling where labels are printed and immediately applied to products or packaging. There are two main market types for Print and Apply Labeling: Automatic Labeling and Semi-Automatic Labeling. In Automatic Labeling, the process is fully automated, from label printing to application, making it ideal for high-volume production settings. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic Labeling involves some manual intervention, such as manually placing the product on the labeling equipment before the label is applied.</p></p>
