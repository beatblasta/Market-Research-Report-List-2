<p><p>Steel powder market can be categorized into three main types: atomization, reduction, and others. Atomization process involves converting molten steel into fine powder through rapid cooling by gas or water. Reduction process involves reducing iron ore into powder form using chemical reactions. The category of "others" includes various methods such as electrolysis and mechanical alloying. Each type of steel powder production method has its own unique advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
