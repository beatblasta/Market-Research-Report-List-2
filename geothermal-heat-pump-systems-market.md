<p><strong>The Geothermal Heat Pump Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Closed Loop</li><li>Horizontal Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geothermal heat pump systems come in different types based on the design of the ground loop used to transfer heat. The Vertical Closed Loop system involves drilling deep boreholes to circulate liquid through vertical pipes. The Horizontal Closed Loop system uses horizontal trenches filled with pipes buried underground. The Open Loop system utilizes water from a well or other water source to transfer heat. Each system type offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs and conditions of the installation site.</p></p>
