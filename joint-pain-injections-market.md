<p><p>Joint Pain Injections market includes two main types: Corticosteroid Injections and Hyaluronic Acid Injections. Corticosteroid Injections are anti-inflammatory medications that provide pain relief by reducing inflammation in the joint. They are often used for short-term pain relief. On the other hand, Hyaluronic Acid Injections provide lubrication and cushioning in the joint, helping to reduce pain and improve joint mobility. Both types of injections are commonly used in the treatment of arthritis and other conditions causing joint pain.</p></p>
