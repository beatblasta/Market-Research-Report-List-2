<p><ul><li>Optical Sorting Machine</li><li>Gravity/Weight Sorting Machine</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated food sorting machines are classified into three main types: optical sorting machines, gravity/weight sorting machines, and other specialized machines. Optical sorting machines use sensors and cameras to examine the color, shape, and size of food items to separate them. Gravity/weight sorting machines utilize weight and size differences to separate items based on pre-set criteria. Other specialized machines include machines that use sensors to detect contaminants or foreign objects in food products. Each type of machine plays a crucial role in streamlining food sorting processes in the industry.</p></p>
