<p><p>PVC window market types include Turn & Tilt Windows, Sliding Windows, Casement Windows, and Others. Turn & Tilt Windows have a dual function of tilting inwards for ventilation and turning for easy cleaning. Sliding Windows slide open horizontally, providing a contemporary look and space-saving benefits. Casement Windows are hinged at the side and open outwards, offering a classic style and excellent ventilation. Other types of PVC windows may include fixed windows, awning windows, or custom designs to suit specific preferences and needs.</p></p>
