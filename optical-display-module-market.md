<p><strong>The Optical Display Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prism Scheme</li><li>Curved Mirror (Worm Eye) Solution</li><li>Birdbath Scheme</li><li>Optical Waveguide Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Display Module market includes various types such as Prism Scheme, Curved Mirror (Worm Eye) Solution, Birdbath Scheme, and Optical Waveguide Solution. Prism Scheme utilizes prisms to reflect light, Curved Mirror Solution uses curved mirrors to direct light towards the user's eyes, Birdbath Scheme involves a spherical mirror to reflect images, and Optical Waveguide Solution employs waveguides to guide light towards the user's eyes. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities for different display applications.</p></p>
