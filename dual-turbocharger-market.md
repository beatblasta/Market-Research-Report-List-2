<p><strong>The Dual Turbocharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual turbochargers are popular in the automotive industry, with two main market types - Type I and Type II. Type I dual turbochargers feature sequential turbocharging, where a smaller turbocharger spools up quickly at low RPMs, followed by a larger turbocharger for higher RPMs. Type II dual turbochargers utilize parallel turbocharging, where both turbochargers work simultaneously to provide more consistent power across a wider RPM range. Both types offer improved engine efficiency and performance, catering to different vehicle requirements.</p></p>
