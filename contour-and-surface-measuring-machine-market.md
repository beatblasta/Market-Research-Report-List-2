<p><p>The Contour and Surface Measuring Machine market can be classified into two main types: Contact and Non-Contact. Contact machines use a probe or stylus to physically touch the surface being measured, providing accurate results but potentially causing damage. On the other hand, Non-Contact machines use optical sensors or laser technology to measure surfaces without touching them, offering fast and precise results without any risk of harm. Both types of machines offer unique advantages and are used in various industries for quality control and inspection purposes.</p></p>
