<p><p>Petrol (Gasoline) engines are commonly found in two main types: 2 stroke and 4 stroke. 2-stroke engines are simpler and more lightweight, making them popular for small vehicles like motorcycles and chainsaws. They also have a power stroke for every revolution of the crankshaft. On the other hand, 4-stroke engines are more efficient and have a power stroke every two revolutions. They are typically used in larger vehicles like cars and boats due to their smoother operation and better fuel efficiency.</p></p>
