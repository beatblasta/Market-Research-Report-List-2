<p><ul><li>High-pressure Injection Technology</li><li>Ultrasonic Technology</li><li>Two-fluid Cleaning Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nozzle cleaner market offers three main types of technologies: high-pressure injection technology, ultrasonic technology, and two-fluid cleaning technology. High-pressure injection technology uses powerful jets of water or cleaning solution to remove built-up debris in nozzles. Ultrasonic technology uses sound waves to break down and remove blockages in a non-intrusive way. Two-fluid cleaning technology combines air and liquid to effectively clean nozzles. Each technology offers unique benefits for different cleaning needs in various industries.</p></p>
