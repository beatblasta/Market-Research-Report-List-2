<p><ul><li>Mediator Microbial Fuel Cell</li><li>Mediator-free Microbial Fuel Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microbial Fuel Cells are divided into two main types based on the presence of a mediator substance: Mediator Microbial Fuel Cells and Mediator-free Microbial Fuel Cells. Mediator Microbial Fuel Cells use a mediator substance to facilitate electron transfer between the microbial biofilm and the electrode surface, while Mediator-free Microbial Fuel Cells rely solely on the direct exchange of electrons. Both types have their own advantages and limitations, and are being researched and developed for various applications in the field of bioenergy and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
