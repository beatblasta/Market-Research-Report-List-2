<p><p>Semiconductor image sensors are a crucial component in digital cameras and other imaging devices. There are two main types of image sensors on the market: CCD (charge-coupled device) and CMOS (complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their high-quality images and low noise levels, making them ideal for professional photography. On the other hand, CMOS sensors are more cost-effective, consume less power, and have faster readout speeds, making them popular for consumer electronics and mobile devices.</p></p>
