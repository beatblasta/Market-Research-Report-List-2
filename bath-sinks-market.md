<p><p>Bath sinks come in three main types – wall-mounted, drop-in, and undermount. Wall-mounted sinks are attached directly to the wall, providing a sleek and modern look. Drop-in sinks are installed by dropping the sink into a cutout in the countertop, with the rim resting on top. Undermount sinks are installed underneath the countertop, creating a seamless and clean appearance. Each type has its own advantages and design aesthetic, catering to different preferences and bathroom styles.</p></p>
