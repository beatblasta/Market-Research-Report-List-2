<p><strong>The Metal Forming Machine Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Forming</li><li>Sheet Forming</li><li>Powder Metal Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal forming machine tools market can be categorized into three main types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves the deformation of solid metal into desired shapes through processes like forging, extrusion, and rolling. Sheet forming involves the shaping of thin metal sheets into various components using techniques like bending, deep drawing, and spinning. Powder metal forming utilizes metal powders to create parts through processes like compaction, sintering, and metal injection molding. These three types cater to different manufacturing needs within the metal forming industry.</p></p>
