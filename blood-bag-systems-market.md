<p><p>Blood bag systems market consists of different types based on the number of bags in the system. Single blood bag systems contain one bag for collection and storage of blood. Double blood bag systems include two bags for separating blood components. Triple blood bag systems have three bags for collecting whole blood and separating plasma and platelets. Quadruple blood bag systems consist of four bags for collecting whole blood, separating plasma, platelets, and red blood cells. Other blood bag systems may have additional bags for specific purposes or configurations.</p></p>
