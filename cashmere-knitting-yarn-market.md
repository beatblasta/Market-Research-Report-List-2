<p><p>Cashmere knitting yarn market offers two main types of yarns - carded yarns and combed yarns. Carded yarns are made by separating and aligning fibers before spinning, resulting in a yarn that is less uniform and more fluffy in texture. Combed yarns, on the other hand, go through an additional step of combing to remove shorter fibers and impurities, creating a smoother and more even yarn. Each type of yarn caters to different preferences and knitting projects in the cashmere market.</p></p>
