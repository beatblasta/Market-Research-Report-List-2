<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane binder market can be categorized into Type I and Type II based on their composition and properties. Type I binders are typically isocyanate-based and are used for outdoor applications such as pavements, walkways, and playgrounds due to their durability and resistance to weathering. On the other hand, Type II binders are polyol-based and are preferred for indoor applications like sports flooring and decorative overlays, providing enhanced flexibility and aesthetic appeal. Each type caters to specific requirements and industries, ensuring a diverse range of application possibilities.</p></p>
