<p><p>Liquid Penetrant Inspection Equipment is used to detect surface-breaking defects in non-porous materials such as metals, plastics, and ceramics. This non-destructive testing method involves applying a liquid dye to the surface of the material, which penetrates into any surface-breaking defects. After a specified dwell time, excess dye is removed, and a developer is applied to draw the penetrant out of the defect, making it visible under ultraviolet or white light.</p><p>The Liquid Penetrant Inspection Equipment Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for quality control and inspection of components in industries such as aerospace, automotive, manufacturing, and oil & gas.
