<p><ul><li>Live</li><li>Dead</li><li>Tandem</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Axle Drive Market includes three main types: Live, Dead, and Tandem. Live axles have power delivered to both wheels simultaneously, providing better traction and performance. Dead axles do not receive power and are used mainly for load-bearing purposes. Tandem axles consist of two or more axles connected in a row, sharing the load and improving stability. Each type of axle drive has its own benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the vehicle or application.</p></p>
