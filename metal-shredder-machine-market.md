<p><p>Metal shredder machines are divided into two main types in the market: mechanical shredder machines and shock wave type shredder machines. Mechanical shredder machines use a rotating blade or hammer to shred metal into smaller pieces, while shock wave type shredder machines use high-pressure water jets to break down metal. Both types of shredder machines are commonly used in recycling facilities and metal processing plants to efficiently reduce the size of scrap metal for further processing.</p></p>
