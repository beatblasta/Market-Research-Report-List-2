<p><p>Cardiac interventional therapy devices are used to treat cardiovascular conditions through minimally invasive procedures. Types of devices include cardiology catheters, guidewires, balloon catheters, coronary stents, fractional flow reserve, and intravascular ultrasound (IVUS). Cardiology catheters are thin tubes used to deliver medication or remove blockages in blood vessels. Guidewires help navigate catheters to the right location. Balloon catheters inflate to open blocked arteries. Coronary stents are mesh tubes placed to keep arteries open. Fractional flow reserve measures blood flow, and IVUS provides detailed images of blood vessels.</p></p>
