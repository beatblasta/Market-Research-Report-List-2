<p><p>Synthetic biology tools market comprises various types such as oligonucleotides, enzymes, cloning technology kits, chassis organisms, and Xeno-nucleic acids (XNO). Oligonucleotides are synthetic DNA or RNA molecules used for gene synthesis and editing. Enzymes are proteins that catalyze biochemical reactions, crucial for DNA manipulation. Cloning technology kits aid in gene cloning and expression. Chassis organisms are engineered microorganisms used as a platform for synthetic biology applications. Xeno-nucleic acids (XNO) are synthetic genetic materials with potential for creating novel biological systems.</p></p>
