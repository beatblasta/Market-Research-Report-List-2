<p><p>Commercial Vehicle EGR System stands for Exhaust Gas Recirculation System, which is a technology used in vehicles to reduce emissions of harmful pollutants such as nitrogen oxide. This system works by recirculating a portion of the engine's exhaust gas back into the combustion chamber, where it mixes with the incoming air-fuel mixture. This process helps lower the combustion temperature, reducing the formation of nitrogen oxide.</p><p>The Commercial Vehicle EGR System market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period. The increasing focus on reducing vehicular emissions to meet stringent regulations is driving the demand for EGR systems in commercial vehicles. Additionally, the rising adoption of advanced technologies in the automotive industry and the growing awareness about environmental issues are contributing to the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Commercial Vehicle EGR System market is the development of hybrid EGR systems that combine both high-pressure and low-pressure EGR to optimize performance and emissions reduction. Overall, the market for Commercial Vehicle EGR Systems is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.</p></p>
