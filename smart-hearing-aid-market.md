<p><strong>The Smart Hearing Aid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Behind-the-ear (BTE) Hearing Aids</li><li>In-the-ear (ITE) Hearing Aids</li><li>In-The-Canal (ITC) Hearing Aids</li><li>Completely-In-Canal (CIC) Hearing Aids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart hearing aid market is segmented into different types based on their design and placement in or around the ear. Behind-the-ear (BTE) hearing aids are worn behind the ear and are suitable for all types of hearing loss. In-the-ear (ITE) hearing aids are custom-made to fit in the outer ear. In-The-Canal (ITC) hearing aids are placed partially in the ear canal for a more discreet option. Completely-In-Canal (CIC) hearing aids are the smallest and most discreet option, fitting entirely inside the ear canal.</p></p>
