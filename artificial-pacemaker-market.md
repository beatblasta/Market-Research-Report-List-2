<p><p>The Artificial Pacemaker Market can be categorized into various types based on the purpose and design of the device. Temporary Cardiac Pacing is used for short-term support during procedures or in emergency situations. Single Chamber Cardiac Pacing involves a pacemaker with one lead connected to either the atrium or ventricle. Double-Chamber Cardiac Pacing involves a pacemaker with two leads connected to both the atrium and ventricle. Other types of pacemakers may include advanced features or be customized for specific patient needs.</p></p>
