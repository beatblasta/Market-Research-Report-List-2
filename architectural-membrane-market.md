<p><p>Architectural membrane market types include Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF), Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), Ethylene-Tetra-Fluoro-Ethylene (ETFE), and others. PTFE membranes are known for their durability and weather resistance. PVDF membranes offer high chemical resistance and flexibility. PVC membranes are cost-effective and versatile. ETFE membranes are lightweight and transparent, allowing for natural light transmission. Other membrane types may include materials such as silicones or coated fabrics, each offering unique properties suited for different architectural applications.</p></p>
