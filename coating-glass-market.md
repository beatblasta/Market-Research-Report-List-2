<p><p>Coating Glass Market encompasses various types such as Heat Reflective Glass (Solar Control Glass) and Low-E (Low Emissivity) glass. Heat Reflective Glass is designed to minimize heat transfer and block UV rays, making it an efficient choice for maintaining indoor temperature and reducing energy costs. On the other hand, Low-E glass is coated with a thin layer of metal oxide to reflect infrared heat while allowing visible light to pass through, making it ideal for insulating buildings and reducing heat loss.</p></p>
