<p><p>Managed Data Center Services refer to outsourcing the maintenance and management of a company's data center infrastructure to a third-party service provider. The three main types of services within this market are Managed Storage, Managed Hosting, and Managed Collocation. Managed Storage involves handling and storing data on behalf of clients, while Managed Hosting provides server space and equipment for clients' websites and applications. Managed Collocation involves renting physical space within a data center for clients to house their own servers and networking equipment.</p></p>
