<p><p>Stretch wrap film is commonly used for securing and protecting pallets and goods during transportation and storage. There are two main types of stretch wrap film: Machine stretch film and Hand stretch wrap. </p><p>Machine stretch film is ideal for high-volume operations where pallet wrapping is automated, providing efficient and consistent wrapping. Hand stretch wrap, on the other hand, is convenient for manual wrapping applications that require flexibility and control over the wrapping process. Both types offer protection against moisture, dust, and damage, ensuring safe and secure transit of goods.</p></p>
