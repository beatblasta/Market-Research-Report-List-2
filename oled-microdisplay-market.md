<p><p>OLED microdisplays come in various pixel pitch sizes, including 6.3µm, 9.6µm, 9.3µm, 12µm, and 15µm. The smaller the pixel pitch, the higher the resolution and image quality. The 6.3µm pixel pitch is ideal for applications requiring ultra-high definition displays. The 9.6µm and 9.3µm pixel pitches are suitable for augmented reality and virtual reality devices. The 12µm pixel pitch is commonly used in medical imaging and industrial applications, while the 15µm pixel pitch is popular for thermal imaging and night vision displays.</p></p>
