<p><ul><li>PTC Heater</li><li>Heat Pump Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Heating System market includes two main types of heating systems: PTC Heater and Heat Pump Heater. PTC Heater stands for Positive Temperature Coefficient, which generates heat when an electric current passes through it. This type of heater is efficient and cost-effective. On the other hand, Heat Pump Heater uses a refrigeration cycle to transfer heat from the surrounding air into the vehicle. This type of heater is more energy-efficient but may be more expensive upfront. Both heating systems serve the purpose of keeping electric vehicles warm during cold weather.</p></p>
