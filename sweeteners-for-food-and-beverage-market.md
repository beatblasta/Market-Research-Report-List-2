Inulin is a type of soluble fiber found in plants like chicory root, known for its prebiotic properties. Sugar alcohols like erythritol and xylitol are low-calorie sweeteners derived from sugars. Other sweeteners in this market category may include stevia, monk fruit extract, and artificial sweeteners like aspartame and sucralose.</p></p>
