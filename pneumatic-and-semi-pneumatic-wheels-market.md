<p><p>Pneumatic wheels are filled with air and provide a cushioned ride, while semi-pneumatic wheels have a solid outer layer with air pockets inside for shock absorption. Full Pneumatic (PR) wheels offer maximum shock absorption and are ideal for rough terrain. HD Solid Pneumatic (SPRT) wheels are puncture-proof and durable, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Super-Flex (SU) wheels have a flexible outer layer for enhanced traction and maneuverability. Each type caters to different needs based on terrain and usage requirements.</p></p>
