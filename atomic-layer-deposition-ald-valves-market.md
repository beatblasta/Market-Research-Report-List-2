<p><p>Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) Valves are categorized into two main market types based on their actuation method: Pneumatic Actuated and Electric Actuated. Pneumatic Actuated ALD Valves are controlled by air pressure, making them suitable for high-pressure applications and offering precise control over flow. On the other hand, Electric Actuated ALD Valves are controlled by an electric signal, providing fast response times and easy integration into automated systems. Both types of ALD Valves offer efficient and reliable operation in various industrial processes.</p></p>
