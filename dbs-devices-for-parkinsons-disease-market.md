<p><p>DBS Devices for Parkinson's Disease Market offers two main types: Single-channel DBS and Dual Channel DBS. Single-channel DBS systems use one electrode lead, while Dual Channel DBS systems use two electrode leads for more precise stimulation. Single-channel DBS devices are simpler and less expensive, making them a popular choice for some patients. On the other hand, Dual Channel DBS devices provide more flexibility in programming and are often preferred for patients who require more complex stimulation patterns or have multiple symptoms that need to be addressed.</p></p>
