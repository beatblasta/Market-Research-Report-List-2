<p><ul><li>Full NFC</li><li>ISO/IEC 15693</li><li>ISO/IEC 14443 Type A</li><li>ISO/IEC 14443 Type A+B</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NFC Reader ICs come in various types catering to different market needs. Full NFC ICs support a range of communication types including ISO/IEC 15693, ISO/IEC 14443 Type A, and ISO/IEC 14443 Type A+B. ISO/IEC 15693 ICs are ideal for longer range communication and are commonly used in applications like inventory tracking. ISO/IEC 14443 Type A ICs are suitable for short-range communication and are commonly found in contactless payment systems. ISO/IEC 14443 Type A+B ICs offer a combination of Type A and B functionalities for versatile applications.</p></p>
