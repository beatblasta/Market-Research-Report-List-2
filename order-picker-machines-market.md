<p><strong>The Order Picker Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Order Picker</li><li>Horizontal Order Picker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Order picker machines are commonly categorized into two types: vertical order pickers and horizontal order pickers. Vertical order pickers are designed to operate in narrow aisles and can lift the operator to higher levels for picking. They are ideal for warehouses with limited floor space. Horizontal order pickers, on the other hand, have a lower lift height and are better suited for use in larger, open spaces. Both types of order picker machines are essential for efficiently and accurately picking and organizing inventory in warehouses.</p></p>
