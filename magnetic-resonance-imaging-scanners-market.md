<p><p>Magnetic Resonance Imaging scanners can be categorized into two types based on automation level - Fully Automatic and Semi-Automatic. Fully Automatic MRI scanners are equipped with advanced software that controls the entire imaging process, from positioning the patient to producing final images. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic MRI scanners require some manual input from an operator, such as selecting imaging parameters or positioning the patient, while the rest of the process is automated. Both types have their advantages and are widely used in medical imaging facilities.</p></p>
