<p><p>RF components market consists of various types of components including Filters, Duplexer, Power Amplifiers, Antenna Switches, Modulators & Demodulators, RF Switches, Low Noise Amplifiers (LNA), and others. Filters are used to select desired frequency signals, Duplexers separate transmit and receive signals. Power Amplifiers boost signal strength, Antenna Switches route signals between antennas. Modulators & Demodulators handle signal modulation and demodulation. RF Switches control signal paths, LNAs amplify weak signals, and other components perform various functions to enable efficient RF signal transmission and reception.</p></p>
