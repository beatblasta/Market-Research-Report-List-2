<p><ul><li>Calender PVC Leather</li><li>Rotary Screen Coating PVC Leather</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calender PVC Leather is produced by passing PVC resin through a series of calender rollers to create a smooth and uniform sheet with desirable thickness and texture. On the other hand, Rotary Screen Coating PVC Leather is manufactured by coating a substrate with PVC resin using a rotary screen printing technique to achieve unique designs and patterns. Both types of PVC Leather are widely used in various industries such as automotive, furniture, and fashion for their durability, versatility, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
