<p><strong>The Sensor Toilet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Induction</li><li>Radar Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sensor toilets can be categorized into two main types based on their sensing technology: Infrared Induction and Radar Sensing. Infrared Induction sensors use infrared light to detect the presence of a person and trigger the flushing mechanism. This type of sensor is more common and cost-effective. On the other hand, Radar Sensing toilets use radar technology to detect movement and activate the flushing process. This type of sensor is more advanced and accurate, but also more expensive. Both types offer hands-free operation and improved hygiene in bathrooms.</p></p>
