<p><p>Veterinary trocars are surgical instruments used in various medical procedures in animals. They are classified into different types based on their intended use. Orthopedic trocars are used for procedures related to bones and joints, thoracic trocars are used for procedures in the chest cavity, and laparoscopic trocars are used for minimally invasive abdominal surgeries. Each type of trocar is designed to provide precise access to specific areas of the body, allowing veterinarians to perform procedures accurately and effectively.</p></p>
