<p><p>Analog IC testers come in different types, including wafer testers and packaged device testers. Wafer testers are used to test the analog ICs during the manufacturing process while they are still in wafer form. Packaged device testers, on the other hand, are used to test the ICs after they have been assembled into their final packaged form. Each type of tester serves a specific purpose in ensuring the functionality and quality of analog ICs before they are used in electronic devices.</p></p>
