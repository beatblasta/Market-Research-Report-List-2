<p><p>Plastic trays are commonly categorized into five main types based on the material they are made of: Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET), Polystyrene, Polypropylene, PVC, and Others. PET trays are known for their durability and resistance to heat, making them suitable for food packaging. Polystyrene trays are lightweight and cost-effective, often used for packaging deli and bakery products. Polypropylene trays offer high impact resistance and are commonly used in the healthcare industry. PVC trays are known for their strength and versatility. Other types of trays include biodegradable options or specialty trays with unique properties.</p></p>
