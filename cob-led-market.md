<p><ul><li>Organic Light Emitting Diode</li><li>Inorganic Light Emitting Diode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>COB LED market types can be categorized into Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) and Inorganic Light Emitting Diode (ILED). OLEDs use organic materials that emit light when electricity is applied, offering flexibility in design and energy efficiency. On the other hand, ILEDs use inorganic materials like gallium nitride to produce light, providing higher brightness and longer lifespan. Both types have their unique advantages and are used in various applications such as lighting, displays, and signage.</p></p>
