<p><strong>The PVDF Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVDF Granule</li><li>PVDF Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVDF Resin market is categorized into two main types - PVDF Granule and PVDF Powder. PVDF Granule is a solid form of PVDF resin used in various applications such as pipes, fittings, and coatings due to its excellent properties like high purity and chemical resistance. PVDF Powder, on the other hand, is a finely ground form of PVDF resin used in powder coatings, membranes, and lithium-ion batteries. Both types have their unique advantages and are widely used in different industries for various applications.</p></p>
