<p><p>Battery separator films are essential components in lithium-ion batteries, facilitating the flow of ions while preventing short circuits. The two main types of production methods for battery separator films are the dry method and wet method. In the dry method, polymer materials are dissolved in a solvent and then cast onto a substrate, while in the wet method, the polymer is directly dissolved in a liquid solution. Both methods have their advantages and are commonly used in the manufacturing of battery separator films.</p></p>
