Polyethylene is a versatile thermoplastic known for its strength and flexibility, used in a wide range of applications. Polyoxymethylene, also known as Acetal, is a strong and rigid thermoplastic commonly used in precision parts. Liquid Crystal Polymer (LCP) is a high-performance thermoplastic with exceptional strength and stiffness, often used in electronic components. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is a biodegradable thermoplastic derived from renewable resources, commonly used in 3D printing. Other polymers in the microinjection molding market include Polypropylene, Polycarbonate, and Polyamide, each with unique properties and applications.</p></p>
