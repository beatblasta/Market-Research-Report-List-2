<p><p>Rain gauges and precipitation sensors are used to measure and monitor the amount of rainfall in a specific area. There are two main types in the market: tipping bucket type and weighing type. Tipping bucket gauges have a bucket that tips when a certain amount of rain is collected, providing a measurement of rainfall. Weighing type gauges measure rainfall by detecting the weight of the collected water. Both types are essential for accurate weather forecasting and water resource management.</p></p>
