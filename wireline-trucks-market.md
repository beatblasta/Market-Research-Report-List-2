<p><strong>The Wireline Trucks Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Hole</li><li>Cased Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireline trucks are essential in the oil and gas industry for both Open Hole and Cased Hole operations. Open Hole wireline trucks are used for logging, perforating, and sampling in uncased wellbores to gather crucial data for reservoir characterization and evaluation. On the other hand, Cased Hole wireline trucks are utilized for well intervention, such as plug setting, production logging, and wellbore cleanout, in completed wells with casing in place. These trucks play a vital role in increasing the efficiency and productivity of oil and gas operations.</p></p>
