<p><p>The Aircraft Window Frame Market consists of two main types: Metal Window Frames and Composite Window Frames. Metal window frames are typically made from aluminum or titanium alloys, offering durability and strength. On the other hand, Composite Window Frames are made from lightweight materials such as carbon fiber reinforced polymers, providing a balance between strength and weight savings. Both types of window frames are essential components of an aircraft's structure, ensuring safety and stability during flight. The choice between metal and composite window frames depends on the specific requirements of the aircraft and its operating conditions.</p></p>
