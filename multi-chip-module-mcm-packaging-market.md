<p><strong>The Multi-chip Module (MCM) Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MCM-D</li><li>MCM-C</li><li>MCM-L</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-chip module (MCM) packaging market is divided into three main types: MCM-D (Dual), MCM-C (Chip-Last), and MCM-L (Layer Last). </p><p>MCM-D features two chips stacked vertically, maximizing space efficiency. MCM-C places the chips side by side horizontally, allowing for better heat dissipation and easier testing. MCM-L involves stacking chips on top of each other, reducing the footprint and providing high interconnect density. Each type offers unique benefits and is chosen based on specific requirements such as performance, power consumption, and cost.</p></p>
