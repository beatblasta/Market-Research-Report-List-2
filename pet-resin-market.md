<p><strong>The PET Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saturated Polyester</li><li>Unsaturated Polyester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Both saturated and unsaturated polyester resin markets are commonly used in the production of PET resins. Saturated polyester resins have saturated chemical bonds, resulting in improved strength, durability, and resistance to moisture. Conversely, unsaturated polyester resins contain unsaturated chemical bonds that can be further cured with the addition of a catalyst, making them ideal for applications that require enhanced flexibility and moldability. Both types of resins play a significant role in the PET resin market by offering a wide range of properties to meet varying industry needs.</p></p>
