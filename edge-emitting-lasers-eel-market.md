<p><p>Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) market includes different types such as Distributed Feedback Laser, Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser, Fabry-Perot Laser, and Broad Area Laser Diodes. Distributed Feedback Lasers offer single-mode operation with high spectral purity. Distributed Bragg Reflector Lasers have a grating structure which enhances the efficiency and wavelength stability. Fabry-Perot Lasers are simple and low-cost options with multiple longitudinal modes. Broad Area Laser Diodes have a wide emitting aperture and are suitable for high-power applications. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries like telecommunications, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
