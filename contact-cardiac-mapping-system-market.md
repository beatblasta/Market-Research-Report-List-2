<p><p>Contact cardiac mapping systems are instrumental in diagnosing and treating heart conditions. The market includes different types of mapping systems such as electroanatomical mapping, basket catheter mapping, and traditional endocardial catheter mapping. Electroanatomical mapping provides detailed 3D images of the heart's electrical activity, while basket catheter mapping offers a unique basket-shaped catheter for mapping purposes. Traditional endocardial catheter mapping involves inserting a catheter through veins to map the heart's electrical signals. Each type serves a specific purpose in aiding physicians in accurately diagnosing and treating cardiac issues.</p></p>
