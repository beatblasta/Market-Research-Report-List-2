<p><strong>The Ellipsometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Ellipsometry</li><li>Generalized Ellipsometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ellipsometry is a technique used to measure the thickness and optical properties of thin films on a substrate. Standard ellipsometry is used for simple, homogeneous films, while Generalized Ellipsometry is more sophisticated and versatile, capable of analyzing complex, multi-layered samples. Generalized Ellipsometry allows for the analysis of anisotropic and inhomogeneous samples, providing more detailed information about the optical properties of materials. Both types of ellipsometry are widely used in research, development, and quality control in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, electronics, and thin film coatings.</p></p>
