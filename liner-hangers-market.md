<p><strong>The Liner Hangers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Liner Hangers</li><li>Hydraulic Liner Hangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liner hangers are used in oil and gas wells to hang liners or casing strings in the wellbore. There are two main types of liner hangers - mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical liner hangers are set using mechanical slips and are commonly used in vertical wells. Hydraulic liner hangers, on the other hand, use hydraulic pressure to set slips and are preferred for deviated or horizontal wells. Both types of liner hangers play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity and stability of wellbore during drilling and completion operations.</p></p>
