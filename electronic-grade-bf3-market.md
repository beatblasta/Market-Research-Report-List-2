In Ion Implantation, BF3 is used to introduce impurities into silicon wafers to alter their electrical properties. Plasma Immersion Doping involves using BF3 to dope semiconductor materials by ion bombardment. Epitaxy is a process where BF3 is used to grow thin films on silicon substrates. BF3 is also used in diffusion processes to create junctions in semiconductor devices. Other applications include etching and cleaning in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
