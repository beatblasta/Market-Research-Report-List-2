<p><p>Chemical protective gloves market can be divided into two main types - disposable gloves and reusable gloves. Disposable gloves are designed for one-time use and are typically made of nitrile, latex, or vinyl materials. They are convenient for quick tasks and offer good protection against chemicals. Reusable gloves, on the other hand, are made of durable materials like rubber or neoprene and can be washed and reused multiple times. They are more cost-effective in the long run and provide better dexterity and grip for handling chemicals.</p></p>
