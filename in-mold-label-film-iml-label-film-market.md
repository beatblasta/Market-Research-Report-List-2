<p><p>In-Mold Label Film (IML Label Film) is a type of label that is inserted into the mold before the plastic product is formed. This type of label is commonly used in Blow Moulding, Injection Moulding, and Thermoforming processes. In Blow Moulding, the label is placed inside the mold and then the plastic is blown into the mold to create the final product. In Injection Moulding, the label is placed in the mold and the plastic is injected into the mold to form the product. In Thermoforming, the label is placed in the mold and then heated plastic is formed around it to create the final product.</p></p>
