<p><strong>The Motorcycle Inertial Measurement Unit Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attitude and Heading Reference System (AHRS)</li><li>Inertial Navigation System (INS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Motorcycle Inertial Measurement Unit Sensors market includes two main types of sensors: Attitude and Heading Reference System (AHRS) and Inertial Navigation System (INS). AHRS sensors measure the orientation of the motorcycle in three-dimensional space, providing crucial data on pitch, roll, and yaw. On the other hand, INS sensors combine AHRS data with accelerometers and gyroscopes to calculate the motorcycle's position and velocity. Both types of sensors play a significant role in enhancing motorcycle safety and performance.</p></p>
