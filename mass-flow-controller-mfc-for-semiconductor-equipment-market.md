Thermal MFCs operate based on the heat transfer principle, while Pressure MFCs work by measuring pressure differentials. Both types are crucial for maintaining accurate gas flow rates in semiconductor manufacturing processes, ensuring consistent and efficient operation of equipment. The market for these MFC types is driven by the increasing demand for advanced semiconductor devices with stringent gas flow control requirements.</p></p>
