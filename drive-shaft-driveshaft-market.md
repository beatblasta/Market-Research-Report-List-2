<p><p>Drive shaft, also known as propeller shaft, is a critical component in the automotive industry that transfers torque from the engine to the wheels. There are two primary types of drive shafts: half shaft and propeller shaft. Half shafts are typically used in front-wheel drive vehicles to transfer power from the transaxle to the front wheels. Propeller shafts, on the other hand, are used in rear-wheel drive vehicles to transmit power from the transmission to the rear axle. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient vehicle operation.</p></p>
