<p><p>Sterile medical paper packaging comes in two main types: pure paper packaging and blister paper packaging. Pure paper packaging is made entirely of paper and is commonly used for packaging items like syringes, gloves, and medical garments. Blister paper packaging, on the other hand, consists of a paper backing with a clear plastic blister to hold the medical device securely in place. Both types of packaging are designed to maintain sterility and protect the contents from contamination during storage and transport.</p></p>
