<p><p>Thermal energy storage systems utilize heat or cold storage mediums to store energy for later use. This technology is commonly used in commercial buildings to reduce energy costs by shifting energy consumption to off-peak hours. Flywheel energy storage systems store kinetic energy by spinning a rotor at high speeds, which can be quickly converted back into electrical energy when needed. These systems are commonly used in industrial applications to provide backup power during outages or to stabilize energy grids.</p></p>
