<p><p>Implantable port devices are used for delivering medications, fluids, and blood products directly into a patient's bloodstream. Single lumen implantable ports have one central catheter for medication administration, while dual lumen implantable ports have two catheters for simultaneous infusion of medications and fluids. Single lumen ports are typically used for delivering chemotherapy, while dual lumen ports are used for patients requiring multiple medications or therapies. Both types of ports provide a convenient and efficient way to administer treatments without repeated needle sticks.</p></p>
