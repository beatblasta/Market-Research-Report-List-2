<p><p>Natural rutile can be classified into two main types based on the size of the particles: Coarse-Particle Rutile Ore and Fine Particle Rutile Ore. Coarse-Particle Rutile Ore consists of larger particles and is mainly used in the production of titanium dioxide pigments and welding rods. On the other hand, Fine Particle Rutile Ore contains smaller particles and is preferred for the production of titanium metal and high-quality refractory ceramic materials. Each type of rutile ore caters to specific industries and applications.</p></p>
