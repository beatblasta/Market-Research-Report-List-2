<p><p>Light vehicle clutches market consists of various types, including electromagnetic clutches, friction clutches, and others. Electromagnetic clutches use electromagnetism to engage and disengage the clutch, providing precise control and efficiency. Friction clutches rely on friction between two surfaces to transfer power, commonly used in manual transmissions. Other types of clutches may include hydraulic clutches or centrifugal clutches. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on factors such as vehicle design, performance requirements, and cost considerations.</p></p>
