<p><p>The electric transformer market can be categorized into various types, including Isolation Transformers, Autotransformers, Toroidal Transformers, and other market types. Isolation transformers provide electrical isolation between the input and output, ensuring safety and protection. Autotransformers are more compact and economical, but still provide efficient voltage regulation. Toroidal transformers offer low electromagnetic interference and high efficiency in a compact design. Other market types may include variable transformers, resonant transformers, and distribution transformers, each serving specific needs in different applications.</p></p>
