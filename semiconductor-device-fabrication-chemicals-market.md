<p><p>Semiconductor Device Fabrication Chemicals market is divided into Gas Chemicals, Solid Chemicals, and Liquid Chemicals based on their physical state. Gas Chemicals include precursors like silane and ammonia used in chemical vapor deposition processes. Solid Chemicals comprise materials like photoresists and dopants used for etching and doping processes. Liquid Chemicals are mainly solvents and cleaning agents used for rinsing and cleaning semiconductor wafers. Each type of chemical plays a critical role in the fabrication process of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
