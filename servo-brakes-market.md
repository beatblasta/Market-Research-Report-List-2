<p><p>Servo brakes are a type of braking system that uses a combination of mechanical and hydraulic systems to provide precise and responsive braking control. There are three main types of servo brakes on the market: vacuum servo brakes, pneumatic servo brakes, and hydraulic servo brakes. Vacuum servo brakes use vacuum pressure to assist with braking, pneumatic servo brakes use compressed air, and hydraulic servo brakes use hydraulic fluid. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
