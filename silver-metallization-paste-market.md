<p><p>Front Side Ag Paste and Rear Side Ag Paste are two types of silver metallization pastes used in the solar industry. Front Side Ag Paste is applied to the front surface of solar cells to create electrical contacts and improve conductivity. Both pastes play crucial roles in improving the overall performance and efficiency of solar cells.</p></p>
