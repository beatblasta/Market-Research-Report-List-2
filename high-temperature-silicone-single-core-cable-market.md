<p><p>High Temperature Silicone Single Core Cable is available in two main types; Solid Single Core Cable and Strand Single Core Cable. Solid Single Core Cable is made of a single solid copper conductor surrounded by high temperature silicone insulation. On the other hand, Strand Single Core Cable consists of multiple thin copper strands twisted together and insulated with high temperature silicone. Both types of cables are highly durable and flexible, making them suitable for various high temperature applications.</p></p>
