<p><p>Lined steel pipes are divided into different types based on the material used for lining. PP lined pipes have a polypropylene lining, PTFE lined pipes have a polytetrafluoroethylene lining, and PVDF lined pipes have a polyvinylidene fluoride lining. Each type offers specific benefits depending on the application, such as chemical resistance, high temperature resistance, and corrosion resistance. Other types of lined steel pipes may include PVC or ECTFE lining, each with its own set of unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
