<p><p>The Airway Stent Market includes two main types of stents - Tracheal Stents and Laryngeal Stents. Tracheal Stents are used to provide support and keep the trachea (windpipe) open, commonly used in cases of tracheal stenosis or tracheobronchomalacia. Laryngeal Stents are used to maintain the patency of the larynx (voice box) in cases of airway obstruction, vocal cord paralysis, or tracheoesophageal fistula. Both types of stents play a crucial role in improving respiratory function and quality of life for patients with airway disorders.</p></p>
