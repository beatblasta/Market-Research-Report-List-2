<p><strong>The Semiconductor Etching Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Etching Equipment</li><li>Wet Etching Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor etching equipment market consists of two main types: dry etching equipment and wet etching equipment. Dry etching equipment uses plasma or reactive gases to remove material from the semiconductor surface, providing high precision and fine detail. On the other hand, wet etching equipment involves immersing the semiconductor in a liquid chemical solution to dissolve unwanted materials. Each type of equipment has its advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
