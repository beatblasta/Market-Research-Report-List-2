<p><p>Automotive Exhaust Heat Recovery (EHR) systems utilize waste heat from the vehicle's exhaust to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions. There are different types of EHR systems including Exhaust gas Heat Recovery (EGHR), which uses heat exchangers to transfer energy from the exhaust gas, Rankine cycle systems that use a thermodynamic cycle to convert heat into work, Thermoelectric generators that directly convert heat into electricity, and Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) systems that use a turbine to recover waste heat from the exhaust.</p></p>
