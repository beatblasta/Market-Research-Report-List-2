<p><p>A sandwich panel system is a construction material consisting of an insulating core sandwiched between two layers of metal, typically aluminum or steel. Sandwich panels offer superior thermal performance, reducing energy consumption and carbon emissions in buildings.</p><p>Another trend driving the market is the rising adoption of prefabricated and modular construction methods.
