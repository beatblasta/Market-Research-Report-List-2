<p><p>Impregnated Graphite comes in two main types - Resin Impregnated and Metal Impregnated. Resin Impregnated graphite involves the infusion of resin into the graphite material, making it more resistant to chemical reactions and improving its overall durability. Metal Impregnated graphite, on the other hand, involves the infusion of metals like copper or silver, providing enhanced electrical conductivity and thermal properties. Both types of Impregnated Graphite are used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics for their unique properties and performance benefits.</p></p>
